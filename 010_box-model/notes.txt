BOX MODEL:

All content is in a box.

the content is in the center of it all.
"padding" is between the content and the border.
"border" is around the content and padding.
"margin" is around the content, padding, and border.

problem:
    When changing size of borders, padding, and margin
    ... pushes others into other locations

solution:
    box-sizing: border-box property, next section...
